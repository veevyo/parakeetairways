Parakeet Airways

The local Midwestern airline that cares about your wallet.

Here at Parakeet Airways, we know you won't always be able to shell out hundreds of dollars just for one little flight. That's why our mission is to offer cheap, safe flights for anyone who needs one.  No matter your budget, there will be a Parakeet flight waiting for you. We promise safety on your flight. If you feel unsafe on one of our flights, please contact us on our social media, and we will  take your feedback to heart. At Parakeet, the customer comes first.
