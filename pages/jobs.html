Working for Parakeet

Parakeet Airways would love to have you on our team! In all job positions, we are looking for:

  • Commitment. We would like someone who is interested in working for us in the long term, and is willing to stick with our airline.
  • Loyalty. Parakeet Airways is looking for employees who would choose to ride Parakeet whether they worked for us or not.
  • Sociability. Someone working on the Parakeet crew is expected to be a team player. This means, a potential employee should have good social skills and be courteous to their fellow employees.
You do not necessarily need working experience to apply for some of Parakeet's lower paying positions. What you will need is cooperation skills and a team-building attitude.

Do you have what it takes?
