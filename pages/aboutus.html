Parakeet is...

an airline based in the Midwest, with headquarters in Kansas City, Missouri.

Parakeet is...

a corporation with a focus on the three S's of air travel: safe, serviceable, and swift.

Parakeet is...

a company that loves to put smiles on the faces of its customers.

Why should you fly Parakeet?



Budget-friendly

Parakeet Airways offers competitive prices, especially when compared to larger airlines.

Safe

Parakeet Airways is known for their top-notch safety. When you fly Parakeet, you fly safe.

Since Parakeet is based right here in the Midwest, it's very convenient for those who are flying from the Midwestern area.

Friendly

The Parakeet team prides themselves on friendly customer service.
