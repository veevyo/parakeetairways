Photos


Pixabay

Most of the photos on this website, especially those used at the top of the page and in picture scrolls, were from the Pixabay website. Pixabay is a free stock image website, which also hosts paid images. Pixabay is a unique stock photo site, in that artists can upload their own photos to the site, and dictate whether the photos are free or paid, and whether the artists shall be credited or not. None of the photographs used on this site require accreditation.
