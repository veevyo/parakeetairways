Parakeet Airways Frequent Flyers Program

What will you get out of our frequent flyers program?


Savings

At Parakeet Airways, we know how expensive a flight can be. That's why you have the opportunities to get outstanding savings when you fly with us for a long time. 

Experiences

By promoting offers for flights you may not have otherwise gone on, Parakeet Airways provides an opportunity for you to explore new destinations cheaply and risk-free. 

Seating

Parakeet Airways provides discounts for premier seating, such as discounted first class seats, for those who partake in the loyalty program.
