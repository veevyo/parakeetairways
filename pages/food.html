How does food work on Parakeet Airways?


When you book a flight with Parakeet, your flight ticket pays for the cost of small snacks, including dietary options. On flights that are estimated to last longer than 2 hours, on-flight meals are sometimes offered with an extra fee.
Regular common snacks include peanuts, chips, and other small food items.

Meals 2 meal plans are offered: Basic and Premium.

Basic (Free for first class riders)

Basic meal options cost $9.99, unless you ride first class. Options include:

Chicken sandwich meal: One grilled chicken sandwich with sliced apples, potato chips, and a small salad of your choosing.

Pasta salad meal: One large pasta salad tossed in olive oil, served with sliced celery, potato chips, and a small Caesar salad.

Premium

Premium meal options cost $13.99 for all riders. Options include:

Teriyaki chicken bowl: Delicious Teriyaki chicken cooked in authentic sauce, served with sautéed vegetables, fresh fruit on the side, and a medium sized Caesar salad.

Vegan meal: One tofu burger served with both vegan veggie straws and celery to dip in provided vegan hummus.

Full kosher meal: Grilled fish served with sautéed vegetables and two authentic mezonot rolls

Snacks for Dietary Restrictions We are committed to accommodating your needs.

Diabetes

Our diabetes-friendly snacks include:

  • Yogurt
  • Nuts, such as almonds, cashews, and peanuts
  • Fruit slices
Kosher

Our Kosher snacks include:

  • Veggie Straws
  • Popcorn
  • An assortment of chips
